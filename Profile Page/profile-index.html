About Me


Just a 23 year old Hispanic kid from Stanfield, NC. From the outskirts of Charlotte, Stanfield is a small country town with little to do and where everyone knows each other but it's what I call home. Experienced in a couple things, which includes: cabinet making/spraying(& the touch up work in between the process), soccer officiating, granite work, and a semi-newbie in web development. Mainly trying to get in the front end process, so I've learned a good bit of HTML, CSS, SCSS(SASS), and JQuery but want to be able to deal with whatever complications arise from the back-end process so I learned a good bit of JavaScript, and some PHP to put more quality in the designing process.

Music is what makes a lot of me and EDM, well, that's the heart of it all! Raves, festivals, and of course, PLUR(Peace Love Unity Respect) have had a major impact on me but not as much as sharing every single one of those moments with people you really care about and love. Although most of the raves I've gone to have been alone, I've come out of most of them having met some great people that I will always remember even if it was for a short time. Somehow, even the most random person on Earth can have the biggest impact on your life. Don't let it mislead you though, I can enjoy and jam to a lot of genres like country, classical, oldies, rock, and even heavy metal, but I have to admit that there's probably one genre, and its Spanish equivalent, that I can only tolerate some of it, hip hop & rap. Also reggeaton for its Spanish equivalent of it. Music is a blessing to all of us and I will never judge someone for their choice in music though. There's a lot upsides to everything, even to rap.
